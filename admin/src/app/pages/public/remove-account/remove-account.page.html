Delete Your Account

To delete your account, follow these steps:

  1. Go to Profile in the app.
  2. Select Delete Account.
  3. Confirm your action by filling out the form with the following:
  • Your Email Address.
  • Your Password.
  • Type the phrase "DELETE ACCOUNT".

After filling out the form, press Delete and confirm your action by selecting "I'm sure".

Important: Once your account is deleted, all your personal information will be permanently removed. No personal data of any kind will be retained. The only information that will be preserved are the paystubs generated for administrative purposes.

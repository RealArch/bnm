Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

  • Name: We collect your first and last name to identify you in the system.
  • Email Address: Your email is used for account creation, communication, and verification purposes.
  • Precise Location: Each time you clock in or clock out, we collect your precise location to calculate the total hours worked during the pay period.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for the purpose of providing and improving our services. Specifically:

  • To calculate the total hours worked during a pay period.
  • To ensure accurate clock-in and clock-out records.
  • To communicate with you regarding your account or any updates to the app.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information, including your clock-in and clock-out records, for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or to provide our services (e.g., payroll processing).

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us through the app or via email.

Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated through the app or via email. Please review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@ra-code.com.
